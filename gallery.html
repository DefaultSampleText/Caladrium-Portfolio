Collections and Experiments

This gallery is divided between curated collections and unsorted works. Curated collections show pieces that share a theme, process, or visual direction, while unsorted works act as an archive of experiments, studies, and unfinished ideas.

Artwork Collections

Browse through each collection below. Each group includes a short introduction, followed by the artworks in that set.

Collection

Nightmare

These works were made for an assignment where I had the goal of recreating nightmares I had experienced as a child. There is a theme of wolves and dogs, as I used to have severe cynophobia.

Collection

Forgotten Knights

Experimenting with Photoshop, I wanted to create a knightcore vibe. I used bright colours on top of dark, smoky backgrounds, and depicted strange scenes often involving weapons or knights.

Collection

Experiments With Procreate 1

Inspired by my recent playthrough of The Outlast Trials, I made a couple of artworks using Procreate that used bright colours and had themes of violence and medical trauma.

Collection

Experiments With Procreate 2

I had just gotten back into using Procreate again. I wanted to make bold and striking pieces, with bright contrasting colours and words forming the shape of the central figures.

Collection

Planets

Made in Adobe Illustrator, these pieces are centred around a theme of space and distant planets.

Collection

Ceramic Works

From my time studying ceramics, many of my works either featured themes of malformed bodies or abstract emotions. They were also often named and designed around songs that I was into at the time.

Loose Archive

Unsorted Artworks

These works sit outside the main collections. They include personal images, joke compositions, profile pictures, class projects, and visual experiments that still show part of my creative process.
